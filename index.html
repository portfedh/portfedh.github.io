About Me

I currently work as an investment manager in a Single Family Office in Mexico City. I've worked there for the past 9 years and have had many different roles related to finance and investing.

I started learning programming as a way to automate tasks and streamline processes at work.

Over the past few years I've been building my programming skills to include Python, JavaScript and several of the tools & frameworks used in web development.
