# Salsa Candela Blog Post - Information Gathering Prompt

**Purpose:** This prompt will be used in the Salsa Candela codebase repository to extract all necessary information for creating a professional blog post about the project.

Reuse for creating other blog posts

---

## Project Context

I need to create a blog post about the "Salsa Candela Website" project for my portfolio. This blog post is intended for **business owners and end clients**, not technical recruiters. The focus should be on the business problem, solution, features, and value delivered to customers and business owners.

## Information Required

Please analyze this codebase and provide detailed information about the following aspects:

### 1. Project Overview & Business Context

- **What business problem does this project solve?**
  - What pain points were the business owners experiencing?
  - What were the goals before starting this project?
  - What alternatives were they using before? (if applicable)

- **What is the core business value delivered?**
  - How does this website help the business grow?
  - What specific benefits do business owners get?
  - What benefits do end customers get?

- **Project timeline and scope:**
  - When was the project started and completed?
  - How long did it take to build?
  - Was it built from scratch or migrated from something else?

### 2. Business Features & Capabilities

**For each major feature, provide:**

- Feature name and description
- Business value (why this matters to customers/owners)
- How customers interact with it
- Any unique aspects that differentiate it from competitors

**Features to document (if present in the codebase):**

- Public-facing website features (homepage, menus, galleries, etc.)
- Online ordering/reservations (if applicable)
- Menu management and display
- Event promotion and management
- Customer engagement features (social media integration, newsletter, etc.)
- Admin dashboard features
- Bar-specific features (if this is the bar website)
- Business management features (if this is the admin system)
- Mobile responsiveness and accessibility
- Multilingual support (if applicable)
- Any other significant features

### 3. Technical Implementation (High-Level, Business-Focused)

- **Technology stack overview:**
  - What technologies power the website? (React, Node.js, etc.)
  - Why were these technologies chosen? (business benefits, not just technical)
  - What databases or services are used?

- **Performance & reliability:**
  - How fast is the website?
  - What's the uptime/availability?
  - How many concurrent users can it handle?

- **Security & data protection:**
  - What security measures are in place?
  - How is customer data protected?
  - Compliance with regulations (if applicable)

### 4. Design & User Experience

- **Visual design:**
  - What's the design philosophy/aesthetic?
  - Brand colors, typography, imagery approach
  - How does the design reflect the business's identity?

- **User experience highlights:**
  - What makes the website easy to use?
  - Mobile experience
  - Accessibility features
  - Customer journey through the site

### 5. Key Metrics & Results

- **Quantifiable results (if available):**
  - Number of visitors/users
  - Conversion rates (orders, reservations, etc.)
  - Customer feedback or satisfaction scores
  - Time saved for business owners
  - Revenue impact (if applicable and appropriate to share)

- **Before vs. After comparison:**
  - What changed for the business after this website was launched?
  - Customer experience improvements
  - Operational efficiency gains

### 6. Challenges & Solutions

- **Major challenges faced:**
  - What were the biggest obstacles during development?
  - Business constraints or requirements that shaped the solution

- **How challenges were solved:**
  - Creative solutions implemented
  - Trade-offs made
  - Lessons learned

### 7. Customer/User Testimonials or Feedback

- Any feedback from business owners about the website?
- Customer comments or reviews (if available)?
- Success stories or notable use cases?

### 8. Future Plans & Roadmap

- What features or improvements are planned?
- How will the website evolve to meet future business needs?
- Any planned expansions or integrations?

### 9. Visual Assets Needed

Please identify and provide paths to:

- Logo files
- Hero/featured images for the blog post
- Screenshots of key features/pages
- Flow diagrams or architecture diagrams (if they exist)

### 10. Code Examples (Only If Relevant)

**Note:** Since this is a business-focused blog post, only include code if it demonstrates a unique business value or innovative solution.

- Are there any code snippets that showcase innovative features?

### 11. External Links & Resources

- Live website URL

- Related projects (Admin panel, Bar website, etc.)

- Any press coverage or mentions

### 12. Competitive Advantages

- What makes this website better than competitors?
- What unique features or approaches were implemented?
- How does this position the business in the market?

## Output Format

Please organize the information in a clear, structured format using markdown with:

- Clear section headings

- Include specific examples and anecdotes when possible
